Guild Wars 2 "The Experience so far"

Published: Fri 05 June 2020
By Renz De Leon

In blogging.

tags: gaming

Guild wars 2 is one of those MMOs that have a great standing in its field and it shows regardless if its a game from 2013, the technology and ideas that come from it ages well. It is a breath of fresh air after struggling to find a decent MMO in today's market as it delivers a different approach to MMOs as its main selling point is the world. Just trying to explore the world gives you experience through unexplored areas, vistas which are hard to reach places some of which gives mini puzzles and hearts which are considered as quests on traditional MMOS.

Initially, the main goal of the game is to reach level 80 to complete expansions and progress into the main story line. After which, you choose which path you take.

The world of Tyria is vast and wide but it is never dull as every where you go something is happening.
