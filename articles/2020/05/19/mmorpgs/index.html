MMORPGs

Published: Tue 19 May 2020
By Renz De Leon

In blogging.

tags: gaming

Massively Multiplayer Online Games or MMORPGs ring a special tune to me in which brings out my childhood memories of waking up early just to login into my game and get a head start. Each level, gear and new zone hits my dopamine levels like a crack addict. There's just something about it that makes me want to keep playing and say "ok, what next?"

I've always had a fascination with MMORPGs that immerses you into the unknown multiplayer world and leaves you to discover its riches alone or with your friends. The infinite possiblities of where you can go and progress is limitless to become whatever you want to be. It is a virtual representation of yourself, your decisions that make your character.

It might just be the dopamine that hits me when i get an upgrade, or the world bosses that requires teamwork to overcome or even just the exploring in which the game immerses me into the world that they had built.
